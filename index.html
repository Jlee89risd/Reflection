Reflection 2

The essay was basically telling me the reason why people should keep making hand made webs. Most of the websites these days are focusing on productiveness, neatness and comfort of people who are using. Those might seem like a good movement, but it can be far from people’s creativity. Hand made webs are important because they stay different from the normal commercial webs and keep webs to stay as a form of an art. Reflection 3

Hello World by Taeyoon Choi was a short essay, but it encompass a lot of important contents inside. Computer is part of human's life, but I feel like only few people are really interested in the inside of the computer. The author of this essay also questioned the similar thing and picked abstraction as something that's blocking people from understanding. Computer is made of binary codes which are deeply complicated, but looking at the interface of the computer stops people from thinking about the inside. The author also made an important comparison between city, art and computer. The repeated patterns that are shown in the inside of the computer also shows up quite often in the city planning. It has both visual and structural importance. And it is also shown in the form of arts. In conclusion, looking inside the computer makes people to question about more things than just how computer works.
